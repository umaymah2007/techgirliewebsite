SOFTWARE ENGINEER

United Kingdom
Full Time, Remote, Flexibility
Expected Salary: £189K – £200K a year

JOB DESCRIPTION

  • Maintain and enhance the main system for job application optimization and performance.
  • Debug and troubleshoot bugs and features, incorporating tests and integrations.
  • Participate in sprint reviews and feature design meetings.
  • Contribute to a collaborative and fast-paced engineering team.

EXPECTATIONS

  • Willingness to learn and problem-solve proactively.
  • Initiative in a work-oriented, team-driven environment.
  • Collaborate with UX designers and software developers.
  • Design, implement, test, and maintain high-quality software.

BENEFITS

Competitive salary, 25 holidays a year, 5 paid volunteering days, excellent work environment, personal medical plans, income protection, and life insurance.
